Interactive Experiments

Projectile Motion (Physics)

Click and drag to set the angle and speed of a projectile. Observe the trajectory and calculate range — learn how launch angle and velocity affect motion.

Click & drag to set angle & speed

Pendulum (Physics)

Drag the pendulum bob and release. Watch the swing and period. Learn about oscillation, gravity, and energy transfer in pendulums.

Drag bob → release to swing

pH Simulation (Chemistry)

Add acid or base to see the pH change and color shift. Learn about acidity, alkalinity, and neutralization reactions.

pH: 7

Reaction Rate (Chemistry)

Click to add a catalyst and watch the reaction speed up visually. Learn about factors that influence reaction rates.

Rate: Normal

Osmosis (Biology)

Add or remove water to see a cell swell or shrink. Understand osmosis and the movement of water across membranes.

Cell Size: 50%

Bacteria Growth (Biology)

Feed the bacteria and watch their population increase. Learn about growth patterns, resources, and population limits.

Population: 10

Bridge Stress (Engineering)

Add weight on a bridge and observe deformation. Learn about stress, load distribution, and structural engineering.

About SkyT X Lab

SkyT X Lab is an offline, interactive science and engineering lab where you can learn by doing. Observe physics motions, chemical reactions, biological processes, and engineering principles interactively. Created by Shakthi Krishna, this lab encourages curiosity, experimentation, and hands-on learning.
